About

Raidcore was created to support the Guild Wars 2 PvE community by enhancing the end-game experience with a platform for performance tracking and addon management.

We provide statistics and analytical tools to help players spot mistakes and improve. We likewise strive to be the all-in-one solution for addon development and distribution, ensuring maximum comfort for end-users.
