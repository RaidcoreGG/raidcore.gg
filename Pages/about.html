About

Raidcore started out with a simple launcher for the game. It was supposed to be the all-in-one solution for account, addon and dps log management. Soon after its release, other tools appeared, that were very similar to mine, so I ended up taking it down. Now I have returned with its successor: The Raidcore App.

But it doesn't end there. A whole suite of addons is waiting, along with a platform to develop and distribute mods. 100% ToS friendly.

Along with that comes a platform for raiding statistics to help yourself improve and compare against other players. It also includes Fractals, Dungeons and Strike Missions. See at a glance whether you improved compared to last week and where your issues lie.
