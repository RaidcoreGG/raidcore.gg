Support Us

Being a user of our platform is already more support than we could ask for, but if you wish to contribute even further, you can do so by introducing your friends to our project.

If you wish to support us monetarily, check out the Supporter Benefits page to learn more. If you wish to develop cool stuff with us, check our open positions. You can also contribute to Raidcore systems directly via GitHub.

About

Raidcore was created to support the Guild Wars 2 PvE community by enhancing the end-game experience with a platform for performance tracking and addon management.

We provide statistics and analytical tools to help players spot mistakes and improve. We likewise strive to be the all-in-one solution for addon development and distribution, ensuring maximum comfort for end-users.
