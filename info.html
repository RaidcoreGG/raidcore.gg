About us

Raidcore, formerly known as "Guild Lounge", started out with the vision to create third party tools to enhance your Guild Wars 2 experience.
I think we're doing a pretty good job with that.

The DESKTOP CLIENT tries to make right what others (including ArenaNet) have been doing wrong for so long.
The all-in-one solution to manage your accounts, addons, combat logs and on top of that you also get some handy insights.

In-game ADDONS developed by us, as well as an opportunity for community creators to distribute their own addons.
A central hub to publish addons, whose development just became a lot easier with our Addon Framework.

LOG PARSING finally puts combat data into perspective, generating not only global statistics but also individual skill performance rating.
Compare your performance against any other player in the game!

With Raidcore you can:

  • See global metrics about any and all encounters in the game
  • Look up individual players or guilds and how they perform
  • See what the most frequent squad compositions are
  • See the best performing classes on any given encounter
  • Link all your alt accounts to your Raidcore account to unify all your data

Getting Started

Getting started is easy. Simply create a new account on the website. You will need to provide in addition to your desired login credentials, your Guild Wars 2 API Key to confirm your ownership of a Guild Wars 2 Account.

Raidcore will automatically get your GW2 account name and associated characters. Any encounters that have been uploaded with your GW2 account in them will show up straight away in your Encounters page.

We try to automatically detect if you have previously changed your account name and attribute those encounters to your new name. If that didn't work, please contact us directly and we can sort it out.

Uploading Logs

You don't have to upload or import anything! We work closely together with dps.report, so all your logs are already there.
You can keep using dps.report or simply drop the log file into the browser window, we'll upload it to dps.report for you.

Rating System

We analyse combat logs generated by arcdps and put them into perspective.

To easily understand metrics at a glance, we use the same colour scheme you're already familiar with:

  • Legendary: 99th Percentile
  • Ascended: 90th Percentile
  • Exotic: 80th Percentile
  • Rare: 70th Percentile
  • Masterwork: 60th Percentile
  • Fine: 50th Percentile
  • Basic: 50th Percentile
