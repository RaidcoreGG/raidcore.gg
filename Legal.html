Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Raidcore collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users of the Services.

Summary

  • We use Google Analytics with enabled anonymisation to track with features are most used so that we can plan our updates better.
  • We use Cookies to keep you logged into your account.
  • Your email never gets published and is only used for password resets, supporter rewards and updates to this legal documentation.
  • Your Guild Wars 2 API Keys are used to generate anonymous statistics.
  • Your Guild Wars 2 API Keys are never shared publicly, with the exception of disclosing them privately to ArenaNet developers for debugging purposes.

What Data do we collect

  • Information about your use of this website (Browser Statistics, IP Addresses)
  • Information that you provide for the purpose of registering on the website
  • Information that you provide for the purpose of partaking in surveys
  • Movement Data In-Game (referring to the video game "Guild Wars 2")
  • Data about your "Guild Wars 2" characters
  • Data about the modules and libraries you load into "Guild Wars 2"
  • Data about your device and its system specifications
